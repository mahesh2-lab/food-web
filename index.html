Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our food ordering and delivery service.

Our ordering process is simple:

  1. Browse restaurants in your area
  2. Select items and customize as needed
  3. Add items to your cart
  4. Proceed to checkout
  5. Choose delivery or pickup
  6. Select payment method and complete order
  7. Track your order in real-time

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Cash on delivery (for select areas)
  • Gift cards and promotional credits

All online payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

After placing your order, you can track it in real-time through:

  • The "Order Tracking" section in your account
  • Our mobile app with live GPS tracking
  • SMS updates at key stages of your delivery
  • Email notifications

Our tracking system provides accurate delivery time estimates based on real-time conditions.

Delivery radius varies by restaurant, typically within 5 miles of the restaurant location. Average delivery times are:

  • Standard delivery: 30-45 minutes
  • Express delivery (select restaurants): 15-25 minutes

Delivery times may vary based on order volume, weather conditions, and distance. The app will show an estimated delivery time before you place your order.

Creating an account is easy and takes less than a minute:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Add your delivery address and phone number
  4. Optionally save payment methods for faster checkout

You can also sign up using your Google, Facebook, or Apple account for a quicker registration process.

Order modifications and cancellations are subject to these policies:

  • You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it
  • After the restaurant begins preparing your food, modifications are limited
  • For cancellations after preparation has begun, a partial refund may apply
  • Contact our customer service immediately for urgent order issues

To modify or cancel, use the "Manage Order" option in your account or contact support.

Delivery fees vary based on several factors:

  • Distance from the restaurant to your location
  • Order size (larger orders may qualify for reduced or free delivery)
  • Peak hours may have slightly higher fees
  • Premium restaurants may have higher delivery fees

The exact delivery fee is always shown before you complete your order. Many restaurants offer free delivery on orders above a certain amount, and subscribers to our premium service get free delivery on all orders.

If you experience any issues with your order, we're here to help:

  • Go to "Order History" in your account
  • Select the problematic order
  • Click "Report an Issue" and select the specific problem
  • Provide details and photos if possible
  • Our customer service team will respond within 24 hours

For urgent matters, use our live chat feature or call our support line at (555) 123-4567. We're committed to making things right.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or concerns.
